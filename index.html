Birthday Date App

What is Birthday Date App about?

The app lets you insert your birthday date i.e. day,month and year and also your gender. It then calculates the day of the week you were born i.e. whether you were born on Monday, Tuesday etc. It then gives an Akan name(derived from Ghanian culture whereby children in Ghana are at times given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born and their gender)
